📞 Contact Support
Are all your products 100% authentic? +
Yes, absolutely. Every product sold on SportElite is sourced directly from official brand manufacturers and certified distributors. Each order includes an authenticity certificate.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. Simply initiate your return through your account dashboard, and we'll arrange a free pickup with a full refund within 3–5 business days.
How fast is your shipping? +
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping delivers within 1–2 business days. Same-day delivery is available in select cities. Gold and Platinum members enjoy free shipping.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes! We deliver to over 150 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days depending on the destination and customs processing.
Can I get personalised gear recommendations? +
Definitely! Our sports equipment experts offer personalised consultations. Use the live chat feature or download the SportElite app where our AI recommends gear based on your sport, level, and goals.
